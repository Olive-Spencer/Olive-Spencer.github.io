Family Trail Ride

This tour is fun for the whole family! No bikes? No problem! We provide bikes to all those who don't have them.

The tour is 4 hours long from start to finish and inludes transportation for you and your bikes from our store location.

You will be riding the south cotton wood trail just outside Bozeman.

Price starting at: $150/per person

Advanced Trail Ride

This tour is for the mountain biking enthusiast.

The tour will last 8 hours. A lunch will be provided as well as transportation for you and your bike to and from our store.

You will be riding the lewis and clark caverns trail outside of Bozeman.

Tours run Monday - Saturday at 8am.

Price starting at: $400/per person
